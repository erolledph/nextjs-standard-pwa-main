# Classic Chicken Adobo

Chicken adobo is a beloved Filipino dish known for its savory, tangy sauce and tender meat.

## Ingredients
- 2 lbs chicken thighs
- 1/2 cup soy sauce
- 1/2 cup vinegar
- 6 cloves garlic, crushed
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 tsp peppercorns
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 cup water

## Instructions
1. Combine chicken, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, peppercorns, sugar, and water in a pot.
2. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes until chicken is tender.
3. Serve hot with steamed rice.
