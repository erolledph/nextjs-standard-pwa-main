prepTime: 15 minutes

servings: 4
ingredients: Large shrimp, peeled and deveined - 1 lb, Coconut milk - 1 can (13.5 oz), Garlic, minced - 4 cloves, Onion, chopped - 1/2 medium, Tomatoes, diced - 1 medium, Fish sauce (patis) - 2 tablespoons, Brown sugar - 1 tablespoon, Thai chili peppers, thinly sliced (adjust to taste) - 1-2, Vegetable oil - 1 tablespoon, Salt and black pepper - to taste, Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish) - 2 tablespoons
instructions: 1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add minced garlic and chopped onion, and saut√© until fragrant and softened, about 3-4 minutes.
2. Add the diced tomatoes and cook until they begin to break down, about 5 minutes.
3. Pour in the coconut milk. Stir in the fish sauce, brown sugar, and sliced Thai chili peppers. Bring the mixture to a simmer.
4. Add the peeled and deveined shrimp to the simmering sauce. Cook for 3-5 minutes, or until the shrimp turn pink and are cooked through. Be careful not to overcook.
5. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Adjust sweetness and spiciness as needed.
6. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving. Serve hot with steamed rice.
