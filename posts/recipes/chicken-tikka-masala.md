ingredients: 500g chicken breast cubed, 1 cup plain yogurt, 2 tbsp garam masala, 1 tbsp cumin, 1 can crushed tomatoes, 1 cup heavy cream, garlic, ginger
instructions:
  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, garlic, ginger, and half the spices for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Grill or sear the chicken pieces until charred. Set aside.
  3. In a pot, saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger until soft. Add remaining spices and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Stir in cream and simmer until slightly thickened.
  6. Add the chicken to the sauce and simmer for another 10 minutes. Serve with rice or naan.
difficulty: Medium

Chicken Tikka Masala is a dish consisting of roasted marinated chicken chunks (chicken tikka) in spiced curry sauce. The curry is usually creamy and orange-coloured. It is one of the most popular dishes in the UK and widely loved around the world.
