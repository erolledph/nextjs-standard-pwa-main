---
title: Mushroom Risotto
date: 2025-12-09
author: Chef Giovanni
excerpt: Creamy Italian rice dish cooked slowly with white wine, broth, and earthy mushrooms.
tags: italian, rice, vegetarian, dinner
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1476124369491-e7addf5db371?w=800&q=80
prepTime: 15
cookTime: 30
servings: 4
ingredients: 300g Arborio rice, 500g mixed mushrooms sliced, 1L vegetable broth, 1/2 cup white wine, 1 onion, butter, parmesan cheese
instructions:
  1. Bring broth to a simmer in a separate pot.
  2. Sauté mushrooms in butter until browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, sauté chopped onion until translucent.
  4. Add rice and toast for 1-2 minutes until edges are translucent.
  5. Deglaze with white wine and stir until absorbed.
  6. Add hot broth one ladle at a time, stirring frequently, waiting for liquid to absorb before adding more.
  7. When rice is tender but firm to bite (about 18-20 mins), stir in mushrooms, a knob of butter, and parmesan.
difficulty: Hard
---

Risotto requires patience and attention. The constant stirring releases the starch from the Arborio rice, creating the signature creamy texture without adding cream.
