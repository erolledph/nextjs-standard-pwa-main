# Easy Vegetable Curry

A simple and delicious curry for vegetarians and vegans alike.

## Ingredients
- 2 cups mixed vegetables
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp curry powder
- 1 can coconut milk
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Saut√© onion and garlic in oil until fragrant.
2. Add vegetables and curry powder, cook for 5 minutes.
3. Pour in coconut milk, simmer for 15 minutes.
4. Season and serve with rice or naan.
