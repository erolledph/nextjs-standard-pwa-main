# Creamy Mushroom Pasta

A quick and delicious pasta dish with a creamy mushroom sauce.

## Ingredients
- 300g pasta
- 250g mushrooms, sliced
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
2. Saut√© garlic and mushrooms in olive oil until soft.
3. Add cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
4. Stir in parmesan, add cooked pasta, and toss to coat.
5. Season and serve.
