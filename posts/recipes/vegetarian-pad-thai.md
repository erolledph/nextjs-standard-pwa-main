excerpt: A tangy, sweet, and savory stir-fried noodle dish packed with tofu, bean sprouts, and peanuts.
tags: thai, vegetarian, noodles, stir-fry

prepTime: 20
cookTime: 10
servings: 2
ingredients: 200g rice noodles, 200g firm tofu cubed, 2 eggs, 1 cup bean sprouts, garlic, chives, crushed peanuts, Pad Thai sauce (tamarind, soy sauce, sugar)
instructions:
  1. Soak rice noodles in warm water until pliable but not mushy (about 20-30 mins).
  2. Fry tofu cubes in oil until golden brown. Push to the side.
  3. Scramble eggs in the wok.
  4. Add noodles and a splash of water. Stir fry until noodles soften.
  5. Add the sauce and toss everything together until noodles are coated.
  6. Add bean sprouts and chives, toss for 30 seconds.
  7. Serve topped with crushed peanuts and lime wedges.
difficulty: Medium
---

Pad Thai is a stir-fried rice noodle dish commonly served as a street food and at most restaurants in Thailand. This vegetarian version swaps fish sauce for soy sauce or a vegan fish sauce substitute to keep the authentic flavor.
