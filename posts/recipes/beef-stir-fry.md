# Quick Beef Stir-Fry

A fast and tasty beef stir-fry loaded with colorful vegetables.

## Ingredients
- 1 lb beef sirloin, sliced
- 2 cups mixed vegetables
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. Toss beef with cornstarch, salt, and pepper.
2. Saut√© garlic in oil, add beef, and cook until browned.
3. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
4. Stir in soy sauce and oyster sauce, cook for 2 more minutes.
5. Serve hot with rice.
