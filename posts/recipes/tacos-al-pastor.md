---
title: Tacos al Pastor
date: 2025-12-09
author: Chef Carlos
excerpt: Mexican street tacos featuring marinated pork and pineapple, served on corn tortillas.
tags: mexican, tacos, pork, street-food
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1551504734-5ee1c4a1479b?w=800&q=80
prepTime: 40
cookTime: 20
servings: 6
ingredients: 1kg pork shoulder thinly sliced, 1 pineapple, achiote paste, chili powder, vinegar, corn tortillas, onion, cilantro
instructions:
  1. Marinate pork in a blend of achiotechili powder vinegar and pineapple juice for at least 4 hours.
  2. Grill the pork slices on high heat until charred and cooked through.
  3. Grill pineapple slices until caramelized.
  4. Chop the pork and pineapple into small pieces.
  5. Warm corn tortillas on the grill.
  6. Fill tortillas with pork and pineapple. Top with chopped onion, cilantro, and salsa.
difficulty: Medium
---

Tacos al Pastor are inspired by Lebanese shawarma but with a distinct Mexican twist. The pineapple adds a sweetness that cuts through the richness of the spiced pork.
