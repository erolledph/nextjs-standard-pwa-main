title: Greek Salad (Horiatiki)
date: 2025-12-09
author: Chef Eleni
excerpt: A refreshing and healthy salad with tomatoes, cucumbers, olives, and a block of feta cheese.
tags: greek, salad, vegetarian, healthy
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1540189549336-e6e99c3679fe?w=800&q=80
prepTime: 15
cookTime: 0
servings: 2
ingredients: 3 ripe tomatoes, 1 cucumber, 1 red onion, 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, 200g feta cheese block, oregano, olive oil
instructions:
  1. Cut tomatoes into wedges and cucumber into chunks. Slice onion thinly.
  2. Place vegetables in a bowl with the olives.
  3. Place the block of feta cheese on top.
  4. Sprinkle generously with dried oregano.
  5. Drizzle a good amount of high-quality extra virgin olive oil over everything.
  6. Serve immediately with crusty bread.
difficulty: Easy
---

Traditional Greek Salad, or Horiatiki, is a staple of Greek cuisine. Unlike many other salads, it contains no lettuce. The focus is on the quality of the fresh vegetables and the olive oil.
