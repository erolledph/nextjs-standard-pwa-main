title: Spicy Tuna Roll Bowl
date: 2025-12-09

excerpt: Deconstructed sushi roll in a bowl. Rice topped with spicy tuna, avocado, and nori.
tags: japanese, seafood, rice, spicy
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1579584425555-c3ce17fd4351?w=800&q=80
prepTime: 20
cookTime: 20
servings: 2
ingredients: 2 cups sushi rice (cooked), 200g sashimi-grade tuna cubed, 1 tbsp sriracha, 1 tbsp mayonnaise, 1 avocado sliced, nori strips, sesame seeds
instructions:
  1. Cook sushi rice and season with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Let cool slightly.
  2. Mix tuna cubes with sriracha, mayonnaise, and a drop of sesame oil.
  3. Divide rice into bowls.
  4. Top with the spicy tuna mixture and sliced avocado.
  5. Garnish with shredded nori and sesame seeds.
  6. Serve with soy sauce and wasabi on the side.
difficulty: Easy
---

This bowl gives you all the flavors of a spicy tuna roll without the need for rolling skills. It's a quick and satisfying meal that is also visually appealing. Ensure you use sashimi-grade fish for safety.
