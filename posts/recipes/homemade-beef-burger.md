---
title: Homemade Beef Burger
date: 2025-12-09
author: Chef John
excerpt: The ultimate juicy beef burger with melted cheddar, crisp lettuce, and secret sauce on a brioche bun.
tags: american, beef, burger, dinner
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1568901346375-23c9450c58cd?w=800&q=80
prepTime: 15
cookTime: 10
servings: 2
ingredients: 300g ground beef (20% fat), 2 brioche buns, 2 slices cheddar cheese, lettuce, tomato, pickles, mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard
instructions:
  1. Form the ground beef into 2 patties. Season generously with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Heat a cast-iron skillet or grill to high heat.
  3. Cook burgers for 3-4 minutes on one side until a crust forms. Flip.
  4. Top with cheese and cook for another 2-3 minutes for medium-rare to medium.
  5. Toast the buns on the grill.
  6. Assemble the burger with sauce, lettuce, tomato, patty, pickles, and top bun.
difficulty: Easy
---

There is nothing quite like a homemade burger. Using good quality beef with the right fat content (at least 20%) ensures a juicy patty. Don't overwork the meat when forming patties to keep them tender.
