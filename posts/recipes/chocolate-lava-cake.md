title: Chocolate Lava Cake

prepTime: 15
cookTime: 12
servings: 2
ingredients: 100g dark chocolate, 100g butter, 2 eggs, 2 egg yolks, 50g sugar, 2 tbsp flour
instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). Grease and dust two ramekins with cocoa powder.
  2. Melt chocolate and butter together in a microwave or double boiler.
  3. Whisk eggs, yolks, and sugar until pale and thick.
  4. Fold the chocolate mixture into the eggs.
  5. Sift in the flour and fold gently until just combined.
  6. Pour into ramekins and bake for 10-12 minutes. The edges should be firm but the center soft.
  7. Invert onto plates and serve warm with vanilla ice cream.
difficulty: Hard
---

Chocolate lava cakes, also known as molten chocolate cakes, are a popular dessert that combines the elements of a flourless chocolate cake and a soufflé. The key is to bake them just enough so the outside is set but the inside remains liquid.
